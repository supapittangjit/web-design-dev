How to Play

You can't play game if you not signed in

You can only select your symbol on the grid in your own round.

You will see your opponent's symbols in the grid as to where they are placed, but not what symbols are placed.

You can select special items before placing the symbol.

Eye item: can see the symbol of the opponent.
Defense items: can protect your own symbol so that it cannot be seen by the opponent.

The last player to place the same symbols in the same row wins.
